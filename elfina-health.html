The part nobody tells you

Most people don't quit therapy. They quit a bad match.

Here's what usually happens. You finally decide to get help. You search "therapist near me," scroll a directory of identical profiles, and pick someone whose photo felt safe. Two sessions in, it's not clicking. You don't have the words for why. So you tell yourself "maybe therapy just isn't for me", and you stop.

It wasn't therapy. It was the match. Picking a therapist by proximity and a friendly headshot is guesswork. The things that actually decide whether you'll open up (their approach, their style, their lived experience, whether they've sat with what you're carrying) are exactly the things a directory never shows you.

That first-match coin-flip is the single biggest reason people drop out before therapy ever has a chance to work. So we stopped leaving it to chance.

How matching works

A method, not a directory.

Five steps between you and a therapist who fits. No scrolling, no guessing. That part is our job.

1

A real conversation, first

You start with a free call with a Therapy Experience Manager, not a form. They listen for what's actually going on, what you've tried, and what you want therapy to feel like.

2

40+ variables, weighed

Our algorithm reads your needs against every screened therapist: approach, specialisation, communication style, lived experience, and dozens of signals a directory filter never captures.

3

A human makes the call

The algorithm narrows the field; a person makes the decision. Our team reviews the shortlist and chooses the match, the nuance you can't reduce to a score.

4

Your therapist is briefed

Before you say a word, your therapist already knows your context. No spending session one, and your money, repeating your story from scratch.

5

You begin, and we stay close

You meet your therapist for the first session. If something feels off, you tell us, and we rematch you. Free. The fit is our responsibility, not yours.

Before you ask

The questions everyone has.

How quickly will I be matched?

Most clients have a therapist within 48 hours of their consult call. The consult itself can usually be booked within a day or two of reaching out.

What if my match isn't right?

You tell us, and we rematch you, free. The fit is our responsibility. You'll never be left to restart the search alone, and you won't pay twice for us getting it right.

How much does it cost?

The matching consult is free. After that you pay per session at standard therapy rates: no subscription, no platform fee, no lock-in. You can pause or stop any time.

Are sessions online or in person?

Sessions are online, so you can see the right therapist regardless of which city they're in. Your match is chosen for fit, not just for postcode.

What can I bring to therapy?

Anything that's weighing on you: anxiety, burnout, relationships, grief, big transitions, or simply feeling stuck. Your consult helps us understand it so we can match accordingly.

Is this confidential?

Completely. Your consult, your context, and your sessions are private. We share only what helps your therapist support you, and only with you knowing.
